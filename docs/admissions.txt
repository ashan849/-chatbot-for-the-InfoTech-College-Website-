Admission Process:

1. Application:
   - Submit the online application form on our website.
   - Provide previous academic transcripts and ID proof.

2. Entrance Test:
   - Required for BBA and Data Science Diploma.
   - Online test covering logic, mathematics, and communication.

3. Interview:
   - Conducted virtually for shortlisted candidates.
   - Assesses motivation and career goals.

4. Offer & Enrollment:
   - Successful applicants receive an admission offer via email.
   - Pay enrollment fee within 7 days to confirm seat.

Important Dates:
- Spring Intake: Applications open January 1st, close March 15th.
- Fall Intake: Applications open July 1st, close September 10th.
