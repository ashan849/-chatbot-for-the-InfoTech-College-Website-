Student Services:

- Career Guidance: Resume building, interview prep, job fairs.
- Library Access: Online journals, e-books, research databases.
- Student Clubs: Tech Club, Debate Society, Entrepreneurship Circle.
- Counseling: Academic and personal support available free of cost.
- Housing Support: Assistance in finding off-campus accommodation for international students.
- Alumni Network: Lifetime access to alumni events and professional networking.
